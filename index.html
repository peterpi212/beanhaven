Our Story

‚òï

Founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiasts Sarah Chen and Marcus Bradford, BeanHaven started as a small weekend market stall in Manchester. What began as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving business serving thousands of coffee lovers across the UK.

Today, we operate our own roastery in the Northern Quarter, where our skilled team crafts exceptional coffee while maintaining the personal touch that made us special from day one.

Our mission is simple: to bring joy to your morning routine with coffee that's not just excellent, but ethically sourced and sustainably produced. Every bag supports farming communities and environmental initiatives.
