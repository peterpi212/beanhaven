Our Story

Where passion meets expertise

Glow & Co was founded in 2019 by Emma Richards, a stylist with over 15 years of experience in some of London's top salons. After returning to her hometown of Oxford, Emma dreamed of creating a space where luxury meets warmth.

Today, our team of skilled stylists and beauty therapists share Emma's passion for making every client feel their absolute best. We believe great hair and beauty isn't just about looking good - it's about the confidence you carry with you.

Whether you're here for a quick trim or a complete transformation, you'll leave feeling pampered, refreshed, and ready to take on the world.
